Bankee App

Spend your money smartly

The problem

Have you ever had no idea about where your money went at the end of each month?

After studying in university or working in society, you had to take care of your life on person, so mostly you start to handwrite, or download money-related app to track the expense. However, it comes to be 2 problems for you:
① Forgetting to upload every expense.
② Running out of money at the end of each month.
 What should you do to control your financial state easily?

The process is based on Lean UX and MVP design. Most interviews were proceeded online, during quarantine period. From users’ needs and experience helps to define 3W, user flow, wireframe and so on …

Quick outline from 3W

Who is the target user ?

Elsa is an uni student. She got allowance from her parents each month. She overcame the same problem as many beginners of finance might meet: have no idea where the money went at the end of each month. The Bankee app aims to help

Those who want to catch up with their financial state

What will the user do on the app?

Set the budgets for your each expense

A wake-up call budget

Connect to your bank cards

Smart expense track

When will the user use the app ?

Check how much money left within your budget

Manage your budget portion

Check your expense
